      Step 4 · Choose an organization

      Block = discuss Topic A completely, then Topic B. Point-by-point = switch back and forth by category.

      Step 5 · Plan your paragraphs

      Paragraph plan

      Introduction: Hook + background + thesis.

      Body 1: First comparison point or Topic A.

      Body 2: Second comparison point or Topic B.

      Body 3 (optional): Extra point or deeper analysis.

      Conclusion: Restate thesis + why it matters.

      Sentence starters (pick a few)

      Both Topic A and Topic B show...
      In contrast, Topic A focuses on..., while Topic B focuses on...
      One key similarity is...
      A major difference is...
      This matters because...
      Step 6 · Writing checklist
      • I understood the prompt and my purpose.
      • I picked two balanced topics.
      • I listed similarities and differences.
      • I wrote a clear thesis.
      • My paragraphs match my organization style.
      • I used transitions like “similarly” or “on the other hand.”
      • I ended with why the comparison matters.
